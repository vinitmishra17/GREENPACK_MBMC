Towards Zero Packaging Waste in Residential Complexes

The Problem

In high-density residential complexes like those under Mira Bhayander Municipal Corporation (MBMC), the increase in e-commerce deliveries has resulted in a surge of packaging waste — especially non-biodegradable materials. Currently there is no dedicated system for packaging recycling or sustainable delivery management.

Why It Matters

Packaging waste contributes to overflowing bins, environmental pollution, and higher landfill loads. By adopting responsible recycling practices and sustainable delivery awareness, communities can significantly reduce their carbon footprint.

Tips for Sustainable Living

  • Choose minimal packaging products when possible
  • Reuse cardboard boxes and bubble wrap
  • Choose consolidated delivery options
  • Separate packaging waste for recycling pickup
